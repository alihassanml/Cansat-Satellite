    Canset Overview

    More Than Just A Destination

      ESP32-CAM This module serves as the core of our CanSat, providing both processing power and the capability to capture images or video with its built-in camera. NRF24L01 The NRF24L01 module enables wireless communication, allowing our CanSat to transmit data to ground stations or other devices in real-time. GPS Incorporating a GPS module provides precise location tracking, essential for navigation and data collection during the CanSat's flight. Atmospheric Pressure Sensor By including an atmospheric pressure sensor, our CanSat can measure changes in air pressure as it ascends and descends, aiding in altitude determination and atmospheric studies.

      These modules work synergistically to enhance the functionality and capabilities of our CanSat, enabling it to collect valuable data and perform various tasks autonomously or under remote control. Through the integration of these components, our CanSat project aims to contribute to the exploration and understanding of the Earth's atmosphere and beyond.
